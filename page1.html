l'Espace des patrouilles

Corona turns everyone's life upside down. Everyone is going through a difficult time during the lockdowns and restrictions. We feel that young people and students are being forgotten, in their need of social contact. Places where they used to be able to express their creativity, get rid of their energy and have fun, are now cold and deserted. As a represented space, we chose the Scout room of Jobertus. Normally filled with laughter and fun, now alienated, empty and tidy. This space lacks its purpose. We decided to reinforce this uncomfortable feeling by increasing the space out of proportion and depicting it in a public place by means of photographs. In addition, we provide sound and references to people who should be present in the room. Lured by the sound of an atmospheric evening, you end up in an empty space with some indications of life. Disappointment and banality make you think back to life before the pandemic.

“What once felt familiar is now an estrangement”

We chose to advertise our project with a poster. This is most fitting with the theme of our installation. We used a similar lay-out that is used for posters made for parties organized by other scout groups.

You can find our project in the brochure of the FOMU. By doing this we don’t only reach youngsters with our poster, but also other, older generations.
